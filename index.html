About Me

Hi!, my name is Cristóbal, I'm a Chilean Graphic Designer, currently living in Vancouver, Canada. I graduated from the animation concept art program at Vancouver Film School and now I'm studying the Web And Mobile Application Development Co-Op Diploma at Cornerstone College.

Services

Graphic Design

As a Graphic designer with several years in that field, I'd like to bring fresh and interesting visual experiences (branding, editorial, etc).

Production Design

To be able to create worlds, characters, environments or props is a good oportunity to present my production design skills.
